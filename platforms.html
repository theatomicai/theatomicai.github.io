Semantic Retrieval
Infrastructure

The retrieval-augmented backbone underneath every Atomic system. Hybrid lexical + dense search, provenance-aware, cross-lingual, and policy-filtered. Engineered to answer institutional questions — not consumer queries.

  • 01Hybrid search. BM25 + dense vectors + entity graph, fused with a calibrated cross-encoder.
  • 02Provenance, native. Every retrieval carries source IDs, span offsets, and confidence — not bolted on after.
  • 03Multilingual by design. Shared embedding space across EN/ES/PT; query and source decouple cleanly.
  • 04Tenant isolation. Each institution's corpus is segregated, encrypted, and never co-trained.

    <ol class="sales-process-list">
      <li>
        <h3>1. Discovery Sprint</h3>
        <p><strong>Timeline:</strong> 1 to 2 weeks</p>
        <p><strong>You provide:</strong> goals, stakeholders, access to sample data, operational constraints.</p>
        <p><strong>We deliver:</strong> architecture direction, risk map, roadmap, and success metrics.</p>
      </li>
      <li>
        <h3>2. MVP Build</h3>
        <p><strong>Timeline:</strong> 3 to 6 weeks</p>
        <p><strong>You provide:</strong> feedback cadence and domain validation.</p>
        <p><strong>We deliver:</strong> functional MVP, evaluation baseline, and prioritized backlog.</p>
      </li>
      <li>
        <h3>3. Production Hardening</h3>
        <p><strong>Timeline:</strong> 2 to 4 weeks</p>
        <p><strong>You provide:</strong> infrastructure constraints and security requirements.</p>
        <p><strong>We deliver:</strong> observability, QA, access controls, and performance hardening.</p>
      </li>
      <li>
        <h3>4. Deployment and Handover</h3>
        <p><strong>Timeline:</strong> 1 to 2 weeks</p>
        <p><strong>You provide:</strong> rollout owners and final signoff.</p>
        <p><strong>We deliver:</strong> deployment, documentation, team onboarding, and support plan.</p>
      </li>
    </ol>
